Cancellation and Refund Policy



Cancellation Policy

Customers can cancel their order within 30 days of purchase. To request a cancellation, customers must contact inspricquotes@gmail.com and provide their order number.


Refund Policy:



We offer refunds on products/services that are returned in their original condition within 30 days of purchase.


Refunds will be processed within 30 days after receiving the returned item.


Shipping and handling charges are non-refundable.


Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during the purchase.


Exceptions



Customized or personalized items/services may not be eligible for cancellation or refund unless there is a defect or error in the product/service received.


Digital products/downloads are non-refundable once the download/access link has been provided.


Contact Information



For any inquiries regarding cancellations or refunds, please contact us at inspiricquotes@gmail.com
